HOW TO USE

Simple steps to verify image authenticity

1

Upload Your Image

Click the "Choose File" button or drag and drop your image into the upload area. We support JPG, PNG, and TIFF formats for maximum compatibility.

2

Preview & Confirm

Review your uploaded image in the preview window. Make sure it's the correct file before proceeding to analysis.

3

Scan the Image

Click the "Scan Image" button to initiate the AI-powered analysis. Our advanced algorithms will process your image in real-time.

4

Review Results

Examine the comprehensive analysis report including authenticity verdict, ELA visualization, pixel distribution graph, confidence scores, and detailed image metadata.
